About the Project

Our Mission

Ocean Sense was born from a shared concern for the declining health of our oceans. As undergraduate researchers at SLIIT, we recognized that conservation efforts are often limited by a lack of real-time, accurate environmental data.

By combining IoT sensing, AI-driven analytics, and accessible visualization, we aim to put powerful monitoring tools in the hands of scientists, policymakers, and conservationists — making ocean protection smarter and more data-driven.
